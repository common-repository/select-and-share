You also will have the option to follow stats on the texts that are being shared and the reach in each social network.

== Description ==

This plugins makes possible for your readers to share pieces of content from your site in the following social networks: Facebook, Twitter, Orkut, Delicious, Hotmail, LinkedIn, Yahoo!, MySpace, and also on WordPress and on e-mail. You also will have the option to follow stats on the texts that are being shared and the reach in each social network.

== Installation ==

Upload the Select and Share plugin to your blog, Activate it.
